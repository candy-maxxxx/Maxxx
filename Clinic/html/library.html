  1. Open Heart Surgery

    Open-heart surgery, also known as cardiac surgery or cardiovascular surgery, is a medical procedure in which a surgeon opens the chest to access and operate on the heart. This type of surgery is typically performed to treat various heart conditions, including coronary artery disease, heart valve disorders, congenital heart defects, and other cardiovascular issues.
